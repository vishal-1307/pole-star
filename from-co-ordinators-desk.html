Message from the Director

A Center for Excellence & Modern Pedagogy

"Pole Star has become a center for excellence in the field of education within a short span of its existence. Our school is an innovative leader in giving stress-free education to its students. The school prepares the students to meet the challenges of life & moulds them into civilized & better citizens. We help students to identify their talents & develop them to the optimum."

The students are exposed to the right learning environment under the guidance of qualified & dynamic teachers. We welcome your child to our institution. We promise to provide all possible facilities & opportunities to make your child successful in society.

Our belief is that all children should have the opportunity to experience the joy of discovery & develop a love for learning; we do not believe in creating robots that demonstrate rote memory of course content.

The vision of our school is to be acknowledged as an institute which provides quality education with modern teaching techniques with traditional roots attached to it. It provides excellent academics of global choice, emphasizes Indian values and culture, and offers opportunities for the holistic development of every child to face the challenges of a changing world.

Our Key Goals:

  • Achieve academic excellence.
  • Achieve excellence in co-curricular & extracurricular activities.
  • Bind sportsmanship & sense of competition through various activities in & outside the school.
  • Create an awareness of personal well-being of every individual.
  • Develop in each child a sense of responsibility & accountability, and qualities like sharing & caring, respect for a lawful environment, society & for oneself.
  • Ensure everyone is world class, highly motivated and a continuous learner who has good work ethics and is professional in work.
  • Achieve excellence in education through total quality management as a way of life & continuous improvement in all areas.

At last, I express my loving gratitude to all my teachers who have worked hard to make my endeavour successful. Especially, I express my sincere thanks to all well-wishers & worthy guardians for their co-operation & hope. Even in the future, they will keep boosting my morale by extending their full support for the upliftment of the institution.

Mr. Kailash Bhardwaj

Director, Pole Star School

Nurturing values, academic competencies, and leadership in Mithilanchal since 2003. CBSE affiliated (Nursery to 10+2).
