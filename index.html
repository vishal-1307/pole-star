About Pole Star

Rooted in Heritage, Ready for the Future

Pole Star School, Madhubani, was founded with a vision to bring premium, modern, and stress-free education to the children of Mithilanchal. Managed by the dedicated socio-cultural organization Vikas Bandhu, the school focuses on a balanced pedagogy of science, arts, and character building.

We offer comprehensive Day Boarding and separate boys' and girls' residential complexes, ensuring safe, healthy, and values-based development. Over the past 22 years, our students have continuously secured top ranks in CBSE assessments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission procedure?

Admissions require submitting the online or physical enquiry form, followed by an interactive diagnostic interview to evaluate basic aptitude. Required documents include Transfer Certificate (TC), birth certificate, Aadhar, and academic transcripts.

Does the school offer boarding/hostel facilities?

Yes, we provide separate hostels for boys and girls inside the campus with structured study hours, clean dining, and full-time security wardens.

Is Pole Star affiliated with CBSE?

Yes, the school is fully affiliated with the CBSE Board up to Senior Secondary Level (+2 level) in Science and Commerce streams. Our Affiliation number is 330199.
