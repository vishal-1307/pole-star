Evaluation & Assessment Model

Following CBSE's latest guidelines, our evaluations are structured to measure understanding rather than memorization.

We conduct Formative and Summative examinations, supported by periodic classroom unit tests, laboratory practical tests, oral quizzes, and project works.

Regular Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs) are held after every major examination, ensuring parents can track student progress. We also provide remedial classes for students needing additional academic support.

Co-Scholastic & Extra-Curricular Zone

To ensure all-round personality development, the school mandates participation in at least two co-scholastic tracks.

🗣️ Debates & Quizzes

Regular inter-house debate competitions, vocabulary drills, and regional GK quizzes.

🎨 Art, Craft & Music

Fine art workshops, painting, origami, classical/modern music lessons, and school choir.
