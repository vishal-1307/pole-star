Enrollment Guidelines

Admissions to Pole Star School, Madhubani are open to all qualifying candidates irrespective of caste, creed, or nationality.

Prospectus and Application Forms can be collected directly from the school administration desk during working hours (08:30 AM to 02:30 PM) or queried using our online assistant. Eligible candidates are called for a brief diagnostic interview assessing basic competencies.

Grade Eligibility & Age Criteria

Grade / Class Age Criteria (As of 31st March) Evaluation Method
Nursery / Prep 3 Years to 4.5 Years Parent Interaction & Observation
Class I to V 5+ Years to 9+ Years Basic Literacy & Numeracy Check
Class VI to IX 10+ Years to 13+ Years Aptitude Test (Sci, Math, Eng)
Class XI (Senior Secondary) 15+ Years Based on CBSE Class X Board CGPA/Marks

Mandatory Checklist of Documents

The following self-attested documents must be submitted along with the completed admission application form:

  • 📜 Original Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school, countersigned by education authorities.
  • 🆔 Student PEN Number (Permanent Education Number) copy.
  • 📅 Date of Birth Certificate issued by Municipal Corporation/State Authority.
  • 💳 Photocopy of Aadhar Card (Student, Father, and Mother).
  • 🖼️ 6 Recent Passport Size Photographs of the student, and 2 copies of parents/guardians.
  • 🩺 Medical Fitness & Blood Group Report from a registered practitioner.
  • 👥 Category/Caste Certificate (if applicable for SC/ST/OBC).
