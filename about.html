Our History

22+ Years of Educational Excellence in Bihar

Pole Star School, Madhubani was inaugurated on November 14, 2003, with the goal of expanding educational access and infrastructure in the Mithilanchal region.

The school is managed by Vikas Bandhu, a registered socio-cultural society formed by leading academicians and social workers. Over the past two decades, the school has grown from a local primary setup into a premier Senior Secondary (+2) institution affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi.

We follow the latest pedagogical protocols recommended by the board, including smart learning platforms, scientific labs, and character-building house competitions.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide stress-free, value-based education that helps every student achieve academic excellence, scientific temper, and deep-seated ethical values rooted in Indian heritage.

👁️

Our Vision

To emerge as a premier center of modern pedagogy in Bihar, preparing students to be socially responsible, secular, and competent leaders capable of resolving global challenges.

🤝

Our Core Values

We nurture character building, patriotism, environmental responsibility, respect, and academic honesty through continuous assessments and leadership experiences.

Milestones

Our Timeline of Growth

2003

Foundation & Inauguration

Inaugurated on Children's Day, November 14, 2003, by the Vikas Bandhu registered society with class structures up to Class V.

2007

Middle School Expansion

Expansion of infrastructural classrooms and laboratory layouts. Classes upgraded to VIII with computer training modules.

2011

First CBSE Class X Batch

Secured initial CBSE affiliation. The first batch of secondary class students passed with a 100% success rate.

2015

Senior Secondary Upgradation

Affiliation upgraded to CBSE Senior Secondary (10+2) level, offering Science and Commerce streams for classes XI and XII.

2020

Smart Classrooms Rollout

Rollout of smart digital classrooms across all classes and introduction of state-of-the-art residential hostel facilities.
