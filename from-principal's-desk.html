Message from the Principal

Nurturing Potential and Joyful Learning

"Pole Star, giving the true meaning of its name, has been shining in the heart of Mithilanchal since the last 17 years, to guide its children in their life journey. It believes that learning is a joyful experience and enables the learners to find the meaning and significance of life. It helps to discover the unfathomed ocean of knowledge and nurture latent and creative potential in the learners."

It believes that education is not just a means of making one a degree holder; it is the gateway to the art of living, which enables pupils to think, to discover the principles of life, and to correctly evaluate experiences to know the difference between the achievable and the unachievable.

The school feels proud to express that it secures a 100% result in the 10th and 12th board exams and congratulates all the stakeholders when it sees its children who have achieved great success in different fields of life and career and serve the society as persons of high integrity and values. Our students are IIT engineers, doctors, teachers, administrators, sportsmen, singers, dancers, and business professionals.

We know that the school is the backbone of the education system. It is the only institution second to the family which is charged with the responsibilities of socializing children and youth. It is the teacher who has to shoulder the responsibility of becoming a parent substitute as soon as a child enters the school. Sympathetic and skilled teachers encourage the development of socially desirable behaviour and give them a sense of self-esteem and a feeling of security. The school is an orchard where human beings are planted. Like gardeners, teachers nurture small plants to turn them into huge trees.

Pole Star, which is a well-organized school, undertakes social activities and projects. Students are self-governed under proper guidance through community projects, and a well-planned house system is one of the features for socializing pre-adolescent and adolescent pupils. Every student is given chances to take an active part in co-curricular activities and is trained in personal responsibilities, allowing them to be moulded into any kind of life situation to lead a happy life.

The school knows that it is a socially developed person who abides by the rules that govern social groups and who appreciates the value of good government, the importance of home relationships, and the value of co-operative living. It would not be an exaggeration if we say that this institute promises to guide in this life voyage as a Pole Star.

Dr. Bharti Jha

Principal, Pole Star School

Nurturing values, academic competencies, and leadership in Mithilanchal since 2003. CBSE affiliated (Nursery to 10+2).
